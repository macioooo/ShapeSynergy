Shape Synergy

Shape Synergy is a web application that allows users to search for exercises and create workout plans.

  • BMI Checking
    • Discover your Body Mass Index (BMI) effortlessly using our BMI calculator. Input your height and weight, and let our intuitive tool calculate your BMI, providing valuable insights into your current health status and helping you set achievable fitness goals.
  • Workout Plans
    • Take your fitness routine to the next level with custom workout plans designed to meet your specific objectives. Whether you're aiming to build strength, improve endurance, or enhance flexibility, our workout planner empowers you to create and track personalized exercise regimens that fit seamlessly into your lifestyle.
  • Diet Plans
    • Fuel your body with the right nutrition by crafting personalized diet plans tailored to your dietary preferences and fitness goals. From balanced meal plans to targeted nutrition strategies, our diet planner equips you with the tools and resources you need to make informed choices and maintain a healthy diet.
